Cloud： Habitat of Souls

2017
Gold，Silver
Pendants, Brooches

 

In ancient Chinese, it is believed that after people pass away, good souls will live happily in the habitat in clouds with gods.

Now it is an age of information technology. It seems like technology is helping us to build the habitat in clouds to accommodate souls——A cloud server（Virtual Private Server [1]）is a virtual machine similar to a brain that can compute, store and share information without physical constraint.

This series emphasizes that a soul carries all the information of one's whole life. People are confused about death and fear the disappearance of soul, so they hope a habitat of souls, even it is a digital heaven or a pure fantasy.
