Coercive Healing

2017
Wool，Cotton thread, PVC sheet, Iron wire
Accessories

 

A truth we always ignore or even deny is being coerced to heal up is more miserable than living with trauma. 

The most essence of healing is not the extrinsic treatment but the spontaneously inherent reconstruction.

It is almost impossible to recover totally as if trauma has never happened. Trauma always leaves us something physical and mental. Some people may never get over the trauma. In such circumstance we might question the significance of persistent cure and the necessity of healing.
