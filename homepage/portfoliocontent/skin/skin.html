Skin is a notion against racism, sexism and other forms of discrimination and prejudice, which are profound and long-term social issues that modern civilization is devoting to eliminating.

It is unwise to judge people by skin color, gender or nation. We might know about one from one's cultural background, education or social class, but we are not supposed to divide people into different hierarchies by any form of standard. Prejudice and discrimination have caused many tragic consequences in history by dividing people into separate groups and expanding the estrangement between them, such as the Nazi massacre of Jews and the Indian caste system.

Series Skin expresses my hope of a world that is tolerant of everyone, a world where everyone can be the special oneself and be treated equally.
